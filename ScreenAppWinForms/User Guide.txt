Sreenshot-Application v0.4

- To start this app double click the .exe file. Black camera should be visible in system tray. Right click to show menu. 
- Left click Screenshot of entire screen - dialog will pop up to choose to save file location, file name and format.
- Left click Screenshot of user selection - Background window will be loaded you will notice this when screen turns a little darker. Now press left mouse button and move to draw a selection. If you move mouse over selection cursor will change to cross, then press right mouse button and move to move selection. Press enter to save screenshot or click the tip button. Clicking cross button will delete selection. Hit ESC to close Background window.
- Keyboard shortcuts 
F10 - Screenshot of entire screen
F11 - Screenshot of user selection

If you notice bugs or any kind of issues feel free to report this in issues section of the repo.
